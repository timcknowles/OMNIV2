Initial Calcium & pH Check
  • Check iCa and pH on ABG/VBG BEFORE starting CRRT
  • If iCa <0.9 → administer 10 mL Calcium Gluconate (2.2 mmol) infusion
  • Set calcium ratio to start at 1.7 mmol/L of effluent
  • If restarting within 4 hrs of disconnection → keep previous ratio
  • Re-check Ca 30 min after starting and any dose adjustments
Citrate & pH Management
  • Use OMNISET-L or PRO sets only
  • Program citrate ratio as 4 mmol/L of blood — NEVER ADJUST THIS RATIO
  • If pH <7.20 at start → administer 50 mL 8.4% Bicarbonate over 30 min
  • Recheck pH and K+ 3–6 hourly and adjust according to tables
